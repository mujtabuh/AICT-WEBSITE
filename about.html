About Us

How we started

Thrift Vikings began two years ago with a simple idea that originated from a conversation among three friends. Sitting together, we realized how much clothing there was in our wardrobe wore only once or twice, which was now perceived as out-dated under the everchanging nature of trends. That is when one of us gave the idea of selling pre-loved clothes or selling thrift clothes and giving them a new life. Although it started as a fun business, it gradually gained succes, all because of the fact that the idea resonated with the people's minds. Motivated by its popularity, we worked to transform the idea into a thriving online business. Today, the platform is dedicated to help sustain fashion, helping others recycle and find unique & affordable style, all while making a positive impact on the planet.

Thrift Vikings: Transforming pre-loved clothes into sustainable style.

Our Mission

At Thrift Vikings, we believe that every piece of fabric has a story to tell. Our mission is to preserve the history these clothes have and at the same time, help the Planet by limiting waste.
We are passionate about creating a community of individuals with a like thought process that value the rich history and creativity of fashion.
We Promise to work towards a future where the textile and fashion industry is a bit more kinder towards the environment, a future where everyone not only finally protects what they currently exploit, but at the same time make it (fashion) a source of good.

Preserving the past, shaping the future.

Our vision

We envision a world where clothing is not just based around trends, but also about witholding a person's individuality ,sustainibility and living.
Our aim is to be a catalyst in the world whose purpose it to change the minds of people, promoting the idea that second hand clothing is not only stylish but also a vital component towards a sustainable future.
Thrift Vikings devotes itself towards fostering a community that celebrates creativity, self expression and the timeless nature of vintage and thrifted clothes.

Every cloth has a story to tell.
